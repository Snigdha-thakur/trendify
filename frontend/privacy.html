This Privacy Policy (the "Policy") governs your use of the website of Trendify Technologies ("Trendify"), hosted at https://trendifytechnologies.com, and its mobile applications (collectively, "Trendify" or "Platform").

The terms "We", "Us", and "Our" refer to Trendify, and "You", "Your", and "User" refer to you as a user of the Platform. "Personal Information" means any information you provide that can personally identify you, such as your name, phone number, email address, or other linked data. This Policy outlines our practices for collecting, using, and protecting your Personal Information to ensure safe usage of the Platform.

1.

Information
We Collect

We collect Personal Information to provide and enhance our services. The types of information we collect include:

  • (a) Information You Provide: We collect and store information you submit through the Platform, including but not limited to your name, email address, phone number, address, date of birth, gender, PAN card number, and bank account details.
  • (b) Automatically Collected Information: We gather certain information about your interaction with the Platform, such as through cookies, web beacons, or other identifiers. This may include data about your browser, device, or usage patterns when accessing Trendify.
  • (c) Information from Third Parties: We may obtain information about you from partners, affiliates, or service providers associated with us to deliver our services.
2.

Data Collection
on the Platform

Data processing on the Platform is managed by Trendify, the Platform operator. Our contact details are available in the Platform's imprint.

3.

How We Use
Your Information

We use your Personal Information for the following purposes:

  • (a) To deliver and manage the services you request.
  • (b) To refer services offered by our affiliates or partners, if applicable.
  • (c) To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.
  • (d) To respond to your inquiries or support requests.
  • (e) To improve the functionality and user experience of the Platform.
  • (f) To communicate regarding payments, fees, or charges.
  • (g) To inform you about promotional offers, new services, or third-party services we partner with.
  • (h) To troubleshoot issues with services provided to you.
  • (i) To conduct internal analytics and performance reviews.
4.

Controller of
Information

Your Personal Information is collected and/or stored by Trendify, including its authorized representatives, affiliates, or business partners, as necessary to provide our services.

5.

Sharing Your
Personal Information

(a) We may share your Personal Information with service providers, data partners, affiliates, or payment processors who assist in delivering our services. These parties are restricted in how they collect and use your information.

(b) Trendify does not allow unauthorized access to your account or identifiable information. Third parties with whom we collaborate may contact you for specific purposes (e.g., to inform you about services), but they are bound by confidentiality agreements.

(c) We may disclose your Personal Information to government authorities as required by law, such as for identity verification, cyber incident investigations, legal proceedings, or other legal purposes.

(d) For payment processing, Trendify works with third-party providers like Razorpay. If you use Razorpay for payments, relevant data (e.g., name, address, email, order details, payment information) is processed directly by Razorpay and not stored on our Platform. Trendify is not responsible for the use of your information under their privacy policies.

6.

Tracking via
Cookies

We and our partners use cookies and similar technologies to track trends, manage the Platform, enhance marketing efforts, monitor user preferences, and collect demographic data. You may disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may limit access to certain Platform features or services.

7.

Additional
Information

(a) Location Data: If you access the Platform via a telecommunication device and grant location permissions, we may collect precise or approximate location data from your device (e.g., via IP address or when the Platform is active).

(b) Device Information: We may collect details about your device, such as model, operating system, version, preferred language, unique identifiers, advertising IDs, serial number, motion data, or network information.

8.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your Personal Information stored in our database. Access is restricted to employees or contractors who require it to perform their roles, such as customer support staff. However, Trendify is not responsible for intercepted information transmitted over the internet, and you release us from claims related to unauthorised use of such information.
